Manufacturing

Real-Time OEE Dashboard — Discrete Manufacturer

+
Manufacturing
Operational Visibility
Southern Ontario
Placeholder

Challenge: Plant management had no real-time visibility into production efficiency. OEE was calculated manually from shift logs — inaccurate and always a day behind.

Solution: Live OEE dashboard pulling directly from the plant's existing PLCs, with automated shift reports and alarm trending for both operators and management.
