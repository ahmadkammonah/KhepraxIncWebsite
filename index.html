Production Line Automation — Food Manufacturer

+

Challenge: A regional food manufacturer was operating a partially manual production line with inconsistent throughput and frequent unplanned stoppages.

Solution: PLC-based automation with SCADA visibility, replacing manual control points and integrating upstream and downstream processes end-to-end.

Placeholder outcome — e.g. 28% reduction in unplanned downtime within 90 days of commissioning.
Mining

Control System Modernization — Processing Facility

+

Challenge: An aging DCS at a mineral processing facility was end-of-life with no vendor support, and failure risk was increasing during active production.

Solution: Phased migration to a modern control platform — parallel commissioning, full I/O validation, operator training — with zero production interruption.

Placeholder outcome — e.g. Successfully migrated 400+ I/O points with zero unplanned downtime during cutover.
Manufacturing

Real-Time OEE Dashboard — Discrete Manufacturer

+

Challenge: Plant management had no real-time visibility into production efficiency. OEE was calculated manually from shift logs — inaccurate and always a day behind.

Solution: Live OEE dashboard pulling directly from the plant's existing PLCs, with automated shift reports and alarm trending for both operators and management.

Placeholder outcome — e.g. Management gained same-shift visibility into production losses for the first time.
